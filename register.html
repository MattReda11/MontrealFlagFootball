Registration Details:

  • Proof of age is required upon registration.
    Please provide us a copy of a government issued ID when picking up your equipment.
  • You are placed on a random team that is in need of the position you prefer.
    If you prefer a team, let us know and we will see what can be done
  • Personal equipement not included. (cleats, gloves, etc.)
  • Jersey and flags are provided to you.(Jersey depends on the team you join)
  • Discount code for Rousseau Sports emailed to you upon registration.

Registration Details:

  • Proof of age is required for each player on the team.
    Please provide us copies of a government issued ID when picking up the team equipment.
  • Every Team must have their own unique name, one will not be provided to you.
  • Personal equipement not included. (cleats, gloves, etc.)
  • Jersey and flags are provided to every member of the team.
  • Discount code for each team member at Rousseau Sports emailed to you upon registration.
