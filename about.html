League Information

Montreal Flag Football League (MFFL) is a flag football league located on the island of Montreal in Quebec, Canada. The league had its first season debut in the summer of 2019. Since then, the number of teams per season has doubled! We now have 12 teams that all face-off against each other in an exciting 10 week round-robin showdown.

Our league is a men's only league with an age restriction of minimum 18 to maximum 30 years of age. Our goal in doing so is to provide a very competitive league format. Our field dimensions are 50 yards long (plus 8 yards end zones on each end) and 30 yards wide.

F.A.Q.

How long does the season last?

The season always starts on the first saturday of July and last 10 weeks.

Are matches cancelled if it rains?

No! We have dedicated indoor facilities in case this happens.

Do you offer any food and/or beverages?

Yes! We have a snack bar on the premise.

Can I bring my dog?

Yes, but he must be on leash at all times..
